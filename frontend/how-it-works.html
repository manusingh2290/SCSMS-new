How It Works

1
Citizen Registration
Citizens register and log into the SCSMS platform using secure authentication.
2
Submit Complaint
Submit complaints with photos, location data, and detailed descriptions instantly.
3
Admin Review
City administrators review complaints and assign them to appropriate workers.
4
Worker Assignment
Assigned workers receive real-time notifications and location details.
5
Resolution & Confirmation
Citizens receive confirmation once the issue is resolved with before/after photos.
