Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register for SCSMS?
Click "Register" on the login page, fill in your details, verify your phone/email, and you're ready to start submitting complaints!
2. What types of complaints can I submit?
You can report issues like:
  • Road repairs
  • Garbage collection
  • Street lighting
  • Water supply problems
  • Drainage cleaning
  • Public infrastructure maintenance
  • …and other civic service complaints.
3. Do I need to take photos of the issue?
Yes! Photos with location help workers understand and resolve issues faster. Multiple photos are supported.
4. How long does it take to resolve complaints?
Most complaints are resolved within 24–72 hours. You will receive real-time updates on status changes.
5. Can I track my complaint status?
Absolutely! All your submitted complaints appear in your dashboard with live status updates and worker assignment details.
6. Is my personal information secure?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption and follow strict privacy policies. Your data is never shared with unauthorized parties.
7. Do I need to pay for using SCSMS?
No, the service is completely free for citizens. Some premium features for businesses may be introduced later.
8. Can I edit or cancel a complaint after submission?
Yes, you can edit details or cancel a complaint from your dashboard before it is assigned to a worker.
9. What if my complaint is not resolved on time?
You can escalate the issue through the "Escalate" button in your dashboard. Our team will prioritize it immediately.
